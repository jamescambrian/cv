About Me

After years working in the UK as Print and Web Designer I made the move to Berlin in 2008.

In the beginning I worked as a freelancer, mostly on behalf of UK charities like Dogs Trust, Blue Cross and smaller agency based clients. I have had a pretty broad career path and have been involved in a wide spectrum of projects from branding exercises to purely developmental projects and with clients ranging from blue chip companies to tiny record labels.

Between 2013 and 2016 I was employed full-time at Delivery Hero Holding GmbH, a Berlin based food ordering start-up. During my roles as a Senior UI designer and web developer I had the pleasure of working on wide array of projects from marketing and internal communications tools up to to product design.

Though I began my role as a designer/developer, my last year of working at Delivery Hero saw me take a leadership role. I helped build a small development team — 3 front-end developers and 1 full-stack developer — and led the team in building a scalable fully responsive UI framework based on Gulp, Sass, Javascript.

I am back working as a freelancer, despite having enjoyed focusing strictly on development, getting my hands dirty, building processes and structuring the team and best practices I now feel I need to go back to what I love: designing, finding solutions and developing digital products and prototypes.

I am a passionate all-rounder looking for a projects where I can use my experience and which allow me the freedom of movement between design and development which I enjoy most.
